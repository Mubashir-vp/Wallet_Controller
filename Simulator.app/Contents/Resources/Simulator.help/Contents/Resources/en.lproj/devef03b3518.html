Simulate drag and drop on iOS

  1. Click and hold on the desired item and wait for the lift animation.

  2. Move the item slightly until the drag item appears under the pointer.

  3. Hold the Control key and release the pointer button.

    A circle appears to show the location of the first finger. The pointer now controls the second finger.

  4. Navigate to the drop destination using the pointer.

  5. Move the pointer over the circle representing the first finger and click to resume control of the drag item.

  6. Move the drag item to the drop target and release the button to finish the drag-and-drop action.
