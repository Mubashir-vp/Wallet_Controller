Interact with iOS

Simulate gesture

Use the pointer, keyboard, and menu items to make gestures on a simulated iOS device.

Gesture

To Simulate

Tap.

Click.

Double-tap.

Double-click.

Touch and hold.

Click and hold.

3D Touch.

On a Mac with a Force Touch Trackpad, choose Hardware > Touch Pressure > Use Trackpad Force. Then, click with the desired pressure.

Two-finger gestures.

Press Option to display touch points.

Move the pointer while pressing the Option key to change the distance between the touch points.

Move the pointer and hold the Shift and Option keys to reposition the touch points.

Two-finger drag.

Click and drag while pressing the Shift and Option keys.

Two-finger pinch, zoom, or rotate.

Click and drag while pressing the Option key.

Drag and drop.

Hold the Control key and release the mouse to lock the first finger on the drag item, navigate to the drop target, then click on the drag item to move it to the target.

For more information, see Simulate drag and drop on iOS

Rotate the simulated device to the right.

Choose Hardware > Rotate Right.

Rotate the simulated device to the left

Choose Hardware > Rotate Left.

Set the orientation of the device.

Choose an orientation from the Hardware > Orientation submenu.

Face Up and Face Down do not rotate the simulator window.

Rotate the device automatically when your app requires a particular orientation.

Choose Hardware > Rotate Device Automatically. A checkmark indicates that automatic device rotation is on

Shake the device.

Choose Hardware > Shake Gesture.

Activate Siri.

Choose Hardware > Siri.

Hold the Home button on the device bezel.

Simulate the device buttons

Use a combination of the buttons on the bezel of a simulator and menu items to use a button or switch.

Button

To simulate

Home

Choose Hardware > Home

Click the Home button on the device bezel.

Sleep/Wake

Choose Hardware > Lock

Click the Sleep/Wake button on the device bezel.

Volume up

Click the Volume Up button on the device bezel.

Volume down

Click the Volume Up button on the device bezel.

Ring/Silent

Click the Ring/Silent button on the device bezel.
