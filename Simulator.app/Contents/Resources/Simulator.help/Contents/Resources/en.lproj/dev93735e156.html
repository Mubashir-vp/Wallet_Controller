Send data to a simulator from your Mac

Use the Share menu on your Mac to send images, locations, routes, and web pages to a simulator. The behavior of the simulator depends on what kind of data is shared or dragged onto it.

Data type

Simulator action

Image

Add the image to Photos. Opens Photos if it is not the current app.

Location from Maps

Sets the location of the device to the shared location.

Route from Maps

Opens Maps with the selected route.

Website URL

Open the URL in Safari.

Send data using the Share button

  1. Open or select the item that you want to send to the simulator.

  2. Click on the Share button.

  3. Choose Simulator from the menu.

  4. Choose the desired simulator from the dropdown menu in the dialog that appears.

  5. Click the Send button.

    The data item is sent to the simulator. The behavior of the simulator depends on the type of data.

Send data by dragging it into the simulator

  • Select the item on the Mac and then drag and drop it onto the desired simulator. The behavior of the simulator depends on the type of data.
