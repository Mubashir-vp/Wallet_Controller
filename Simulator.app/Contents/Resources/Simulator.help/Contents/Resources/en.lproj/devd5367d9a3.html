Test Apple Pay

Use Simulator to test the flow of Apple Pay in your iOS and watchOS app.

  1. From your app, present a payment sheet.

  2. Choose Hardware > Authorize Apple Pay.

  3. Simulator calls paymentAuthorizationControllerWillAuthorizePayment and then paymentAuthorizationControllerDidAuthorizePayment with the payment details, data from the sheet, and an empty encrypted blob.

See alsoPassKit API documentation
