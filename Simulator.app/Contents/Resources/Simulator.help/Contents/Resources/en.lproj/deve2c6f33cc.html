Install your app on a simulated device

Install your app using Xcode

  • The most common way to install your app on a simulator is by running it from Xcode. For more information, see Run your app in Simulator from Xcode.

Drag your app onto a simulated device

  1. Build your product in Xcode.

  2. Chose View > Navigators > Show Project Navigator.

  3. If necessary, expand the disclosure triangle icon () next to your project to show the Products folder for your project.

  4. Expand the disclosure triangle next to the Products folder in the Project navigator.

  5. If the name of the product is shown in red, build your app.

  6. Drag the app from the Products onto a simulator to install the app.

Use the Finder Share menu

  1. Chose View > Navigators > Show Project Navigator.

  2. If necessary, expand the disclosure triangle icon () next to your project to show the Products folder for your project.

  3. Expand the disclosure triangle next to the Products folder in the Project navigator.

  4. If the name of the product is shown in red, build your app.

  5. Select the app.

  6. Choose File > Show in Finder to show the location of the app bundle.

  7. Control-Click on the app bundle and choose Share > Simulator in the menu that appears.
